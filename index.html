Interaction Hub

Developmental Roadmap for One-Button Users

Visual Stimulation:
Phase 1: Sensory Agency
Goal: Understand "I press, and something happens." Massive immediate feedback.
💥Neon Burst 🎧DJ Party 🫧Bubble Pop 🎨Neon Canvas
Phase 2: Sustained Action
Goal: Repeated pressing results in continuous movement or progress.
🐍Mega Snake 🏎️Neon Racer
Phase 3: Persistence
Goal: Follow a multi-step sequence to reach a big finale.
🚀Mega Rocket 👾Monster Feed
Phase 4: Timing (Action on Cue)
Goal: Waiting for the right moment. Teaches inhibitory control.
🪵Mega Log Hop
Phase 5: Visual Matching
Goal: Choice making. Scanned selection of matching icons.
🎯Target Match
Phase 6: Logic & Categorization
Goal: Logical grouping. Sort by size, color temp, or category.
📦Logic Sort
Phase 7: Pre-Communication
Goal: Using scanning to request items or express needs.
