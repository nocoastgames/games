Developmental Roadmap for One-Button Users

Music:
Phase 1: Sensory Agency
Understand "I press, and something happens." Pure cause-and-effect.
💥Neon BurstMassive supernova explosions. 🎧DJ PartyTechno beats & pulsing speakers. 🫧Bubble PopCalm, iridescent magic. 🎨Neon CanvasSplash paint on a black wall.
Phase 2: Sustained Action
Repeated pressing results in continuous movement or high-speed travel.
🌌Star TunnelCinematic hyperdrive effect. 🐍Mega SnakeLunge toward the glowing apples. 🏎️Neon RacerPump the engine for a lap trophy.
Phase 3: Persistence
Following a multi-step sequence to reach a big finale.
🚀Mega RocketAssemble and launch to the moon. 👾Monster FeedFeed a goofy monster 10 snacks.
Phase 4: Timing
Waiting for the right moment. Teaches inhibitory control.
🪵Mega Log HopWait for a log to jump the river. 🔔Neon SlamHit the target in the carnival meter.
Phase 5: Visual Matching
Intentional selection. Scanned selection of matching icons.
🎯Target MatchWait and tap the matching item.
Phase 6: Logic & Categorization
Identify logical groups. Sort by size, category, or quantity.
📦Logic SortSort items based on logical groups. 🔢Mega MathIdentify more than and less than.
